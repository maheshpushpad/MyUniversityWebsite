About Group

SAM GROUP OF INSTITUTIONS works under the aegis of SAM
Educational Society in central India, founded by Late Shri I S Saluja
over four decades ago with a vision to offer quality based education.
Since then SAM Group has established and is running successfully,
ten colleges and a school in Bhopal.

The society has ever since established range of educational institutions in the field of Management, Engineering & Technology, Para Medical Sciences, Pharmacy, Nursing, Girls Degree College, B.Ed, Ayurveda, and CBSE affiliated school with modern day facilities and state of the art infrastructures.
